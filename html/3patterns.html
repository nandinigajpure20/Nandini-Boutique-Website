Patterns

Peshwai Pattern

Peshwai is the most royal and rich pattern of Maharashtrian women. All Women in Maharashtra wears peshwai from very ancient times. From backside the design is of Single Kashta .

Mastani Pattern

Mastani pattern is same as Peshwai pattern , the only difference is that it consists of a zig-zac design in the front side . From backside the design is of Single Kashta .

ShaahiMastani Pattern

Shaahi-Mastani is similar to Mastani pattern but it contains more zig-zac plates in its front side .From backside the design is of Double Kashta .

Rajlaxmi Pattern

Rajlaxmi is the most beautiful pattern , it also consists of many zig-zac plates but here the plates are little inverted upside . From backside the design is of Double Kashta .

SemiDevsena Pattern

Semi-Devsena is similar to a normal saree but it has given a little nauvari look which looks very elegant . From backside the design is of Single Kashta .

Devsena Pattern

Devsena pattern came from the movie named Bahubali which consists of a straight long border from the middle in the front side . From backside the design is of Double Kashta .

Dhoti Pattern

Dhoti is very common among boys . Boys wear Dhoti with kurta which gives traditional look to them .

Backside of Nauvari

Single Kashta

The back side of the Nauvari will be Single Kashta in Peshwai pattern, Mastani pattern, Semi-Devsena pattern as well as in Dhoti .

Double Kashta

The back side of the Nauvari will be Double Kashta in Shahi-Mastani pattern and Rajlaxmi pattern also if you want double kashta
in other patterns then that will be considered.
