About Us

Our speciality is to create traditional Maharashtrian outfits and give you all the best traditional look . As the typical Nauvari is very difficult to wear as well as time consuming and it does not contain different patterns ,so to overcome this we are creating Nauvari which will be very comfortable to wear and it has many beautiful patterns which you can select of your choice.
Other than this we are also offering the other styles which girls/women wears .

What do we do ?

We will provide you readymade nauvari of the pattern which you want to stitch of your choice .Other than Nauvari we are also offering you the other styles such as one-piece,patiyala ,ghagra and all other styles .

The Material to make the different patterns of Nauvari is the 9 meter saree . If you want to stitch the Nauvari pattern or the other dresses ,you will have to give/send us the materials of your choice or else you can even tell us which fabric you want and what type of design you want in your Nauvari or dresses .

For contacting us you can just place the below button "Place Order" or else simply go to the Contact us page and then by filling the details in the given form you can place the order. You can even visit our Boutique or call us or else Mail us on the given mail Id and give us the proper details of your orders .
